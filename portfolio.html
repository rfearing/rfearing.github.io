Blue Trunk Foundation

Blue Trunk is a web application built to bridge the gap between travel and accessibility.  Users can filter business search results to fit their unique needs, making travel simple and accessible for everybody.

I engineered a solution for Blue Trunk's vison of enabling individuals  with disabilities to have the resources and information available to make travel more accessible.

My role: Team Lead, Solutions Engineer, Lead Developer

Technologies

  • React.js & Redux
  • Node.js / Express
  • Heroku & AWS S3
  • Elasticsearch

Features

  • Custom Search Tool
  • User Accessibility Profiles
  • Business / User interaction portal
